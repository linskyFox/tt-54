You can do it by following these easy steps:

Step 1

Sign in Your Account, to Register and (Create Your Quotation Request).

Step 2

Name Your SKU and Quantity.
SKU is an unique index number for each product. You can find SKU# in product page.
Quantity - is the quantity you want to buy for this item. If items were descripted as packs, sets, you'd better let us know how many packs or sets you want.
We also can help you sourcing the special product you like if we don't have in our website

Step 3

Make Sure Delivery Places.
The shipping charge will be different in line with package weights & delivery places. Please let us know your country and city, so we can ensure that we get the right shipping charge. If you Got a forwarder of your own, we will contact them for shipping.

Step 4

Give A Deadline
For some orders there might be a deadline, like "my package should be arrived before.....". If your order has this requirement, please let us know so we can make sure we will catch that time. All in all, if you submit the necessary info as more accurate as possible, We will be able to find the right price for you as soon as possible.

Step 5

Leave Your Name and Contact Information (please include your telphone number, skype, email address) and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
