Discount Coupon

Rules for Coupon Use:
* Coupons are NON-transferable, NON-refundable and NOT redeemable for cash.
* Coupons CANNOT be combined. One coupon only can be used for one order. Besides, a coupon can be applied to the same item, excluding special products in the same order. For example, if a customer orders a product A1 with a special discount of 10% and product A2, coupon is only available to product A2.
* Coupons must be activated within the specified promotional period.
* Coupons can be used on all items on TOMTOP.com unless specific usage rules. Specific rules of coupon usage vary with promotion. Please see the usage rules for the relevant

Promotion for more details.
* To redeem the coupon, choose the corresponding coupon you obtained in the coupon box when checkout.
* In case of order cancellation, the coupon will be available for next order before the coupon expired.

Restriction on Coupon Usage
* Coupons can be used on specified categories or specified products, such as all items on electronic.
* Coupons can be used within valid date. For example, a coupon remains valid until March 31, 2011.
* Coupons are subject to the minimum order amount (the amount of special offers are not included). e.g., the order reaches to $100.
* Quantitative restriction on coupons for every customer. For example, one customer can use a coupon once only.

How do I use TOMTOP's coupons?
When you find an available coupon and you want to use it, just click the provided link on the shopping website. And then you will get a "coupon code". When checkout, with this Provided "coupon code", you can get your discount automatically applied to your order.

* Illustrations below directly show the procedure of using a coupon code: Get an available coupon code and use it during the checkout.
* Review your discounts in detail before consummating the order.

Coupon on Refunds

* Coupons are invalid if the buyer makes a Refund Request and the supplier agrees to refund the total amount of the order.

* If a buyer is entitled to a refund, the refund amount will NOT exceed the buyer's cash payment (e.g. a US $5 coupon was used on a US $20 order. Refunds will not exceed US $15).
