Benefits Concierge Program

All VIP Buyers will benefit from our Customer Service Concierge Program. Special members of our Customer Service team will track all VIP Buyer orders, handle disputes, and solve any potential problems before they happen.

In addition, VIP Buyers are guaranteed extremely fast service and if the VIP Buyer requires any assistance, he or she may contact the Concierge Service for help.

All VIP Buyers will receive special discounts on some items with our VIP Exclusive Price.

In addition to the VIP Exclusive Price, VIP Buyers will also receive extra discounts on TOMTOP promotions.
