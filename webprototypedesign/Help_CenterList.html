What Does "Expected Restock", "Out Of Stock" And "Sold Out Currently" Mean?
Here are the usual stock messages you can see on the product pages and their corresponding meaning;
"In stock, usually dispatched in X business day" – We have current stock of the item and it usually is sent within the given days. If you are ordering multiple products that are packaged together then the highest number of business days will be the expected dispatch time.
"Expected restock on 12th June 2014" This is the date of expected stock to arrive. This is an estimate and we do not guarantee that there will be stock available at this time. This date may change if we experience delays from our manufacturers.
"Sold out currently" – We have no stock available and we cannot estimate the next shipment arrival date.
"Out Of Stock" – The stock is sold out and we are not expecting to restock, usually this means we will stop selling this product.
Will I Get An AC Adapter For My Country?
Yes you will be provided an adapter to suit your country; you still need to be aware the product matches your local AC voltage. A 110V product will not work in a 240V country, a product labeled 110-240v or 240v product will. Please be sure to check the voltages on any electrical products you order.
